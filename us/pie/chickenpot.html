Pie 23: Chicken Pot

2023-11-30

Recipe is from All Recipes. I took the advice of the top comment.


Taste: 5

Difficulty: 4

Vibes: 6

Suggested pairings: apples & cheese

In the realm of comfort foods, chicken pot pie holds an undeniable place. Nothing says homey like flaky crust, juicy chicken, and tender vegetables (none cruciferous) smothered in gravy. Katie Beth in particular enjoys them and had wanted us to make one since this project began. Well here it is!

It hasn’t been the quietest week and a half for us, which is why I am only now posting our pie--five days past the end of the week! I was sick for several days and have a big exam tomorrow morning, so this isn’t going to be super long.

We decided to use a tried and true flaky crust for the top and bottom. The recipe I linked seemed pretty good, but I also took the advice of the top comment for several things, including the addition of some potatoes to the onions, carrots, celery, and peas.

I also added a bit more chicken broth even on top of the chicken gravy based on advice from other commenters. I think it did improve the outcome.

The evening of the pie, we invited some friends over from church. They were bringing their baby, so I used some extra dough and filling to make a tiny baby pot pie. Turns out the child preferred fruit anyway, but everyone else very much enjoyed the pie.
