Chocolate Chip Peanut Butter Pumpkin Protein Cookies (2C 3P Cookies)

Yield: 6 cookies Calories: 76 kcal each Protein: 7 g each

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup pumpkin puree (120 g)
  • 1 scoop vanilla whey protein (30 g)
  • ⅓ cup peanut butter powder (35 g)
  • 1 whole egg
  • 3 tbsp erythritol (or another 1-for-1 sugar substitute)
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp pumpkin pie spice
  • ¼ tsp ginger
  • Pinch salt
  • A few dark chocolate chips (~15 g)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Mix the pumpkin puree, erythritol, and egg.
  3. Separately mix the whey, peanut butter powder, baking powder, spices, and salt.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Mix well.
  5. Use a cookie scoop to drop onto baking sheet. I made 6 largish cookies. They will spread.
  6. Drop the chocolate chips on top of the cookies.
  7. Bake 10–12 minutes on a middle rack, stopping while they are still moist.
  8. Enjoy warm or cool!
