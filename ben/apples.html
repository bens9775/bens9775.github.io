Custom Apples to Apples

I really enjoy Apples to Apples so I made a version with red cards referencing the Honors Community at Union Univeristy. Here's how you can print your own copy or create an expansion pack.


To Print Your Own

Below are PDFs for the base game red cards, the green cards, and the first expansion pack of red cards. Assuming you are a Union student, download them and email them to printing@uu.edu. Ask for them to be printed with two sheets each on 11x17 white paper, cardstock (no thinner than #110). For a few extra bucks they will cut the cards apart for you. I also linked the rules if you want to print a copy.

To Make An Expansion Pack

It took me a while to make the template so I'm sharing it with you. It's a file for a free and open source program called LibreOffice Draw. If you download this software you can just open the templates below, replace the text for each section of the card with your text, and export as a PDF. I have also included the PDF of the green back and of the red back. For University Printing to easily make these cards, you need to make one long PDF for each color. If you are printing 90 cards, there will be 10 fronts and 10 backs, alternating. See the finished PDFs above. I used the free and open-source program PDFsam to combine the pages. It isn't hard and you can use any PDF merging software.
