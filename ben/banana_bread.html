Extraordinarily Moist Banana Bread

Ingredients:

CreamBanana MashDry Ingredients
1 stick room temperature salted butter3 very ripe bananas250 g all purpose flour (2 cups)
230 g brown sugar (1 cup)1 tbsp milk1 tsp baking soda
3 large eggs1 tsp cinnamon1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt

Steps:

1. Preheat oven to 325 F. Butter a 9x5 baking pan.

2. Cream butter and sugar until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, mixing after each.

3. Seperately fork-mash the peeled bananas. Add milk and cinnamon (pumpkin pie spice may be substituted).

4. Seperately mix dry ingredients.

5. Add bananas to cream and stir until combined.

6. Add dry ingredients, mixing until flour disappears.

7. Pour into pan, optionally sprinkling cinnamon sugar on top.

8. Bake at least 70 minutes, probably more. Cool in pan for 15 mintes. Remove from pan and cool completely on rack before slicing.
